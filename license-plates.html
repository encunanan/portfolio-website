UTAH LICENSE PLATES

As part of a campaign to promote marine education and conservation, I designed a series of specialty license plates for the state of Utah featuring three eye-catching animal designs: an octopus, a penguin, and a shark. These animals were selected based on guest favorites at the aquarium, making the designs both meaningful and memorable to the community.

Created to stand out on the road, each plate is bold, colorful, and crafted to spark curiosity while representing the mission of the aquarium I work with. The public is invited to vote for their favorite design, and the winning plate will be presented to Utah state legislators for potential adoption as an official specialty plate. This project brings together illustration, audience engagement, and real-world impact—turning everyday vehicles into moving ambassadors for ocean life.
