DRONE WORK

I am an FAA Drone certified under Part 107! I've worked most with a DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise, and one of my favorite parts of being able to fly a drone is that it's a skill that I've gotten to hone in on little by little. To me, its gratifying to pilot the drone, review the footage, and figure out the best way to adjust the flight technique to get the desired shot.

For this, I've had the opportunity to work not just on my flight skills, but also my photography and videography. I've worked closely with one of my coworkers on camera settings and composition to help me get stronger at this. We earned our certification together, so its great to be able to work closer together to develop these skills at the same time.

The cover photo for this section was taken by my coworker and friend Syd White!
