HELLO, I'M ELLI!

Hi there! I’m a California native who's been lucky to call Utah home for the past seven years. I earned my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design from the University of Utah, along with minors in Animation Studies and Computer Science. More recently, I completed my MBA through Western Governors University.

As a designer with a background in both fine arts and business, I’m passionate about the intersection of brand identity, effective marketing, and data-driven strategy. My MBA has shaped the way I approach creative work, seeing design not just as visual storytelling, but as a strategic tool that supports clear, measurable outcomes. I focus on creating thoughtful, insight-driven design that helps brands connect with their audiences and communicate with purpose and impact.

Outside of design, you’ll usually find me playing basketball at the park, practicing my digital drawing skills, or hitting the hiking trails in the summer (Big Cottonwood Canyon is my go-to spot). At home, I’m either cheering on the Las Vegas Aces, the University of Utah women’s basketball team, or hanging out with my dog, Jackson.

Thanks for stopping by, I'm excited to connect with you!
